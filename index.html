FAQ

How can I get the adaptive shopping list?
We are hard at work providing you this wonderful app. It will be available shortly in the iOS app store and in the Android market place.
What happens if I change my phone?
The adaptive shopping list uses the cloud to help you track your habit. As long as you have an account, you can get your data back. this means you can change phone as much as you want. You can also change from an Android Phone to an iPhone.
